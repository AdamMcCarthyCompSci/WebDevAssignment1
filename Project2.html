The Curse of Starry Isle

Adventure CTM

Description

This is an adventure map set on a sprawling island with multiple interesting biomes to explore. It is similarly styled to a CTM map, except it has many unique gameplay mechanics to add to the genre.

You begin your adventure in the quaint Starry Village. Quickly, you realise that a curse has fallen upon the island, and you're the hero that's tasked with saving it. Four crystal shards are scattered across the island, and they're protected by various challenges to overcome. It's your job to bring these shards back to the village to unlock the curse. Once the curse is unlocked, a battle must ensue for the fate of the island itself!
